How to Play & Controls
🎮 Basic Operations

To move a piece, you can either Click the piece and then click the target square, or Drag & Drop the piece to its destination square.

Use the controls sidebar to Undo moves, Flip the board layout, or restart a New Game.

🤖 Playing against the Minimax AI

Switching to vs AI mode enables the Minimax engine. The engine computes optimal moves using alpha-beta pruning.

You can choose between three difficulty levels: Easy (incorporates randomized noise), Med (Medium depth search), and Hard (deeper positional lookup).

🏆 Rules, Special Moves & Pawn Promotion

The game supports standard chess rules including checkmate verification, stalemate detection, castling, and en passant captures.

When a pawn reaches the opposite end of the board, an overlay dialog will prompt you to select a promotion piece (Queen, Rook, Bishop, or Knight).
